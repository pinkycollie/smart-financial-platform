# DEAF FIRST - TechFW Pitch Outline

## 1. Introduction (1 minute)
- Brief personal introduction highlighting your experience in real estate, tax, insurance, and finance
- Introduce DEAF FIRST as a platform reimagining financial services and education specifically designed for the deaf community
- Open with a compelling statistic about financial literacy or service gaps in the deaf community

## 2. The Problem (2 minutes)
- Explain how current financial systems are designed for hearing people without considering deaf experiences
- Highlight specific challenges deaf people face:
  * Communication barriers with financial advisors/professionals
  * Limited visual resources for complex financial concepts
  * Lack of ASL-integrated tools for financial decision-making
- Share a brief story illustrating how these barriers lead to tangible financial disadvantages

## 3. Your Solution - DEAF FIRST (2 minutes)
- Present DEAF FIRST platform: "We're not adapting to THEIR system; we're building OUR system"
- Outline core components:
  * Visual-first financial education modules
  * ASL-integrated real estate navigation tools
  * Tax preparation services designed specifically for deaf users
  * Insurance documentation with visual clarity as the priority

## 4. Market Opportunity (1 minute)
- Size of deaf community in your target region
- Estimated financial service needs and current gaps
- Growth potential as you expand services and regions

## 5. Business Model (1 minute)
- How DEAF FIRST will generate revenue
- Pricing structure that ensures accessibility
- Path to sustainability and profitability

## 6. Traction and Roadmap (1 minute)
- Current status (which Sparkyard stage you're in)
- Key milestones already achieved
- Clear 12-month roadmap with measurable goals

## 7. Why TechFW Partnership (1 minute)
- Specific TechFW resources that would benefit DEAF FIRST (reference their programs)
- How your success contributes to their mission of transforming innovative ideas into scalable businesses
- Emphasize community impact alignment with their "give before you get" value

## 8. Call to Action (1 minute)
- What specific support you're seeking now
- Which TechFW program you believe is the best fit (ThinkLab or SmartStart)
- Express enthusiasm for contributing to TechFW's community
