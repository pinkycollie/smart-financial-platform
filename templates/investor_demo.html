The Opportunity

The deaf and hard-of-hearing community has been chronically underserved by traditional financial institutions, creating a significant market gap. Our DEAF FIRST platform addresses this unmet need with specialized services that prioritize visual communication, ASL support, and deaf-friendly design.

With minimal competition in this specialized space and growing awareness of accessibility needs, we are positioned to capture a significant share of this underserved market.
