About Our DEAF FIRST Approach

The DEAF FIRST platform was created by MBTQ GROUP (founded April 26, 2022) to bring accessible financial services to the deaf community. Our platform is used by financial educators, advisors, and agents who serve deaf clients. Every aspect of our platform is designed with deaf accessibility as the primary consideration, not as an afterthought.
