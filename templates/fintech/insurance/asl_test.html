ASL Video Settings

DEAF FIRST Design Approach

Our platform embraces a DEAF FIRST design philosophy, where accessibility is built into the core experience rather than added as an afterthought. Some key features include:

  • Authentic ASL videos from trusted sources
  • Insurance products designed specifically for deaf and hard of hearing individuals
  • Visual-first interface with clear iconography and minimal text dependency
  • Integration with multiple ASL video sources to provide comprehensive coverage
