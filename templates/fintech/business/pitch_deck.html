DEAF FIRST Business Model

Business & Impact Framework

Our DEAF FIRST platform is built on the principle that we're not adapting to THEIR system; we're building OUR system. The business model combines financial empowerment with sustainable revenue streams.

View Impact Data

TechFW Pitch Outline

1. Introduction
  • Experience in real estate, tax, insurance, and finance
  • DEAF FIRST platform reimagines financial services
  • 94% of deaf individuals report communication barriers with financial professionals
2. The Problem
  • Current financial systems designed for hearing people
  • Communication barriers with financial advisors
  • Limited visual resources for complex financial concepts
  • Lack of ASL-integrated tools for financial decision-making
3. Our Solution
Core components:
  • Visual-first financial education modules
  • ASL-integrated real estate navigation tools
  • Tax preparation services designed specifically for deaf users
  • Insurance documentation with visual clarity as priority
  • Multi-embedded insurance services for deaf-specific needs
4. Market Opportunity
  • 11.5 million Americans with hearing loss (3.6% of population)
  • $490 billion in combined spending power
  • 1.2 million consider American Sign Language primary language
  • Growth potential as services and regions expand
