Digital Framework Principles

Our platform is built with five core principles that prioritize deaf accessibility from concept to implementation.

Visual Clarity

High contrast visuals with clear, distinct elements optimized for visual processing—the primary information channel for deaf users.

ASL Integration

Native American Sign Language videos for all important content, not as an afterthought but as a primary communication method.

Visual Feedback

Clear visual cues and feedback for all interactions, eliminating dependency on audio feedback systems.

Spatial Relationships

Information organized with clear visual hierarchy and logical spatial relationships that mirror the grammatical structure of ASL.

Community Input

Continuous feedback from the deaf community at every stage of development, ensuring authentic representation of needs.
