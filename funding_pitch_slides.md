# DEAF FIRST Platform - Funding Pitch Deck

## Slide 1: Title
**DEAF FIRST: Financial Inclusion Reimagined**
*The first comprehensive financial platform built specifically for the deaf community*

## Slide 2: The Team
- **Jane Doe, CEO**: 15+ years working with deaf-owned businesses

## Slide 3: The Problem
- 37 million Americans are deaf or hard-of-hearing
- 78% report inadequate access to financial services
- Financial concepts get lost in translation, even with interpreters
- Average deaf individual loses $3,500 annually in financial opportunities

## Slide 4: The Solution
**DEAF FIRST Platform: A comprehensive financial ecosystem with:*
- Native ASL video integration for all content and support
- Specialized tax preparation with deaf-specific deductions
- Custom insurance products for deaf community needs
- Financial education designed for ASL as primary language
- Credit restructuring with accessible communication

## Slide 5: Technology Differentiators
- **Multi-Provider Video Integration**: Combines Mux, SignASL, and VSL Labs
- **Three-Tiered ASL Approach**:
  - Pre-recorded ASL content library
  - Live ASL technical support
  - Interactive ASL components
- **White-Label Architecture**: Multi-tier licensing system for resellers

## Slide 6: Market Opportunity
- **Total Addressable Market**: $250B in financial services for deaf community
- **Completely Underserved**: No comprehensive solution exists
- **Expansion Potential**: White-label licensing to financial professionals
- **Regulatory Tailwinds**: ADA compliance requirements increasing

## Slide 7: Business Model

- **White-label Licensing**: Tiered fees for professionals (15%)
- **Partner Referrals**: Commission-based referrals (10%)
